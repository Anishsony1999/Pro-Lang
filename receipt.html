About Angasana Velavaru Maldives:

Angsana Velavaru, a five-star resort in the Maldives’ South Nilandhe Atoll, offers a perfect mix of luxury and natural beauty. With 113 beachfront and overwater villas, many featuring private pools, guests can enjoy ultimate privacy and breathtaking ocean views. The resort boasts diverse dining options, serving international and local cuisine in stunning settings. Guests can unwind at Angsana Spa with revitalising treatments or dive into adventure with snorkelling, jet skiing, and catamaran sailing. Committed to sustainability, Angsana Velavaru actively engages in marine conservation, allowing guests to participate in eco-friendly initiatives. Whether seeking relaxation or excitement, this resort offers a seamless blend of world-class hospitality and Maldivian charm.

Room Categories:

Beachfront Villa: Situated along the pristine shoreline, this villa offers a prime beachfront location surrounded by swaying palm trees and the refreshing ocean breeze. Unwind in your private garden at the rear, enjoy al fresco dining, or lounge on the outdoor seating area while soaking in uninterrupted ocean views from your exclusive villa terrace.

InOcean Pool Villa: Perched on stilts above the crystal-clear waters, this two-story villa presents the perfect Maldivian retreat. The rooftop pavilion serves as a tranquil space for in-villa spa treatments, while the wooden poolside deck provides a relaxing spot to admire the vibrant marine life below.
